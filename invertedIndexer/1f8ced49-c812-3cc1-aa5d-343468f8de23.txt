												<p class="introductiontext" itemprop="description">Have you ever wanted to create your smartphone? You've got a unique chance to create it by yourself.</p>
								<p class="introduction">
					Posted by <span itemprop="author" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/Person"><span itemprop="name"><a href="/members/roasterygames" itemprop="url">RoasteryGames</a></span></span> on <time datetime="2018-02-26" itemprop="datePublished">Feb 27th, 2018</time> 				</p>
				<div id="articlecontent" itemprop="articleBody"><p>Have you ever wanted to create your smartphone? <strong>You've got a unique chance to create it by yourself.</strong><br /><br />So soon release of new incredible game on Android!<br /><br /><strong>Smartphone Tycoon</strong> - create your company by producing smartphones. It is an unusual game will interact your in a world of smartphones. You will be able to create ideal smartphone with smart and easy editor!</p>
<p>In a game you'll be able to edit the <em>width</em>, <em>height</em>, <em>thickness</em> of smartphone <em>as you like</em>.</p>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_11.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_11.png" alt="Screen 11" width="1280" height="720" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_12.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_12.png" alt="Screen 12" width="1280" height="720" /></a></p>
<hr />
<p>That is not the <strong>END</strong>! You will have a lot of function in a game:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>choose the color of your smartphone</strong></li>
</ul>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_8.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_8.png" alt="Screen 8" width="600" height="337" /></a></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>choose the screen Technology and Resolution</strong></li>
</ul>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_3.1.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_3.1.png" alt="Screen 3 1" width="1280" height="720" /></a></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>choose camera and even a place on smartphone</strong></li>
</ul>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_14.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_14.png" alt="Screen 14" width="600" height="337" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_1.1.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_1.1.png" alt="Screen 1 1" width="1280" height="720" /></a></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>choose additionally functions</strong></li>
</ul>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_9.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_9.png" alt="Screen 9" width="600" height="337" /></a></p>
<ul>
<li><strong> you even will be able to set a sale price</strong></li>
</ul>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_4.1.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_4.1.png" alt="Screen 4 1" width="600" height="337" /></a></p>
<p><strong>You will have many other things in a game!<br /><br /></strong></p>
<p>You will have your ovn office and what office whithout employees?</p>
<p>In <strong>Smartphone Tycoon</strong> you will be able to employee staff from all planet!</p>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_10.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_10.png" alt="Screen 10" width="600" height="337" /></a></p>
<p>Simulated intellect will allow to make news. Nice or bad news - depends on you.</p>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_7.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_7.png" alt="Screen 7" width="600" height="337" /></a></p>
<p><strong>Try to overtake the volume of sales of big companies as <em>Apple</em> and <em>Samsung</em>!</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_5.1.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_5.1.png" alt="Screen 5 1" width="1280" height="720" /></a></p>
<p><strong>Research additionally functions to create smartphones better.</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_13.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="http://media.indiedb.com/images/members/5/4084/4083471/profile/Screen_13.png" alt="Screen 13" width="1280" height="720" /></a></p>
<p><em>P.S. We don't want to describe all opportunities of game, but trust, you will have something incredible!</em></p>
<p><strong>SMARTPHONE TYCOON will be available for Android in March-April 2018.</strong><em><br /></em></p>
